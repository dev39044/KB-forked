# TOP switches

q - quit
h - help
x - highlight column you are currently sorting by
z - add some colors

m - mem/swap usage. Toggle memory area on/off
l - toggle on/off load area
t - toggle on/off tasks area

B - switch on/off boldness

< and > - these 2 with conjunction of "x" will sort by and highlight corresponding column.

R - reverse 
pgUp, pgDown, arrows - these are used to show you more processes than current window can show.

F - turn on/off columns
